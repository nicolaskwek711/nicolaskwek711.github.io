01 documentary film

behind the facade

a documentary produced for my Introduction to Documentary module.

synopsis

In the heart of Joo Chiat lies a piece of Singapore's culture that can never be replaced — the Peranakan houses of Koon Seng Road. But nobody knows who, or what, is behind the facade. In Behind the Facade, we get to know the history of these structures as told by long-time owner of No.8 Koon Seng Road, Kwek Buck Chua, as he walks us through what's really behind the facade. We get exclusive access into the doors of No.8 Koon Seng Road and get intimate with the rich Peranakan culture it holds within its walls.

02 short film

#dpg

a meet-cute short film produced for my Video Production for Narratives module.

synopsis

A post-it doodle found on a campus bridge is misread and quickly spreads online, leaving Brandon saddled with an embarrassing nickname he never asked for. Nicole, who caused the image to go viral, soon realises her mistake and is forced to confront the harm of a careless post. As she searches for ways to make amends through small, quiet gestures, their paths cross again at the bridge — where a simple drawing offers the chance to start over.

03 reality

ctrl + alt + amon

a reality short film produced for my Entertainment Content Production module.

synopsis

Chosen to lead Singapore Polytechnic's junior team into the legacy-heavy RoboRoarZ competition, first-year engineer Amon balances sleepless nights, schoolwork, and rising pressure as technical setbacks test his limits. When his team falls short on competition day, Amon is forced to confront what engineering — and success — truly mean to him.
